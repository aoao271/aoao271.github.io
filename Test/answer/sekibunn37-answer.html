問題：次の式を計算せよ

  • (1)の答え：\( \dfrac{3}{2} + \log 2 \)

    解説：

    \( \hspace{35px} \begin{eqnarray} \displaystyle \int_{1}^{2} \dfrac{e^{\log (x^2 + 1)}}{x} dx & = & \displaystyle \int_{1}^{2} \dfrac{x^2 + 1}{x} dx \\ & = & \displaystyle \int_{1}^{2} \biggl( x + \dfrac{1}{x} \biggr) dx \\ & = & \left[ \dfrac{1}{2}x^2 + \log x \right]_{1}^{2} \\ & = & \biggl( 2 + \log 2 \biggr) - \biggl( \dfrac{1}{2} + 0 \biggr) \\ & = & \dfrac{3}{2} + \log 2 \end{eqnarray} \)

  • (2)の答え：\( \dfrac{1}{10}e^x(\sin 2x - 2\cos 2x) \)

    解説：

    \( I = \displaystyle \int e^x\sin x\cos x \) とおく。

    \( \hspace{35px} \begin{eqnarray} I & = & \dfrac{1}{2} \displaystyle \int e^x\sin 2x \\ & = & \dfrac{1}{2}e^x\sin 2x - \displaystyle \int e^x\cos 2x \\ & = & \dfrac{1}{2}e^x\sin 2x - (e^x\cos 2x + 2\displaystyle \int e^x\sin 2x) \\ I & = & \dfrac{1}{2}e^x\sin 2x - e^x\cos 2x - 4I \\ 5I & = & \dfrac{1}{2}e^x\sin 2x - e^x\cos 2x \\ I& = & \dfrac{1}{10}e^x(\sin 2x - 2\cos 2x) \end{eqnarray} \)

  • (3)の答え：\( 0 \)

    解説：

    \( I = \displaystyle \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin x}{\sin^3 x - \cos^3 x} dx \) とおく。

    \( \hspace{35px} \begin{eqnarray} \displaystyle \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin x}{\sin^3 x - \cos^3 x} dx & = & \displaystyle \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin (0 + \frac{\pi}{2} - x)}{\sin^3 (0 + \frac{\pi}{2} - x) - \cos^3 (0 + \frac{\pi}{2} - x)} dx \\ & = & \displaystyle \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\cos x}{\sin^3 x - \cos^3 x} dx より \end{eqnarray} \)
    \( \hspace{35px} \begin{eqnarray} I + I & = & \displaystyle \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin x + \cos x}{\sin^3 x - \cos^3 x} dx \\ 2I & = & \displaystyle \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin x + \cos x}{(\sin x + \cos x)(\sin^2 x + \sin x\cos x + \cos^2 x)} dx \\ I & = & \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin x + \cos x}{(\sin x - \cos x)(1 + \sin x\cos x)} dx \\ & = & \dfrac{1}{2}\displaystyle \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin x + \cos x}{(\sin x - \cos x)(1 + \dfrac{1 - \{\sin x - \cos x\}^2}{2})} dx \\ & = & \displaystyle \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\sin x + \cos x}{(\sin x - \cos x)\{3 - (\sin x - \cos x)^2\}} dx \\ & = & \displaystyle \int_{-1}^{1} \dfrac{1}{t(3 - t^2)} dt　(t = \sin x - \cos x, dt = (\sin x + \cos x)dx) \\ & = & \displaystyle \int_{-1}^{1} \dfrac{1}{3t - t^3} dt \\ & = & 0　(\because y = \dfrac{1}{3t - t^3} は奇関数) \end{eqnarray} \)

  • (4)の答え：\( \biggl( \dfrac{1}{12 - 48x^3} \biggr)\log(x^4 - x) +\dfrac{1}{36}\log\biggl| 1 - \dfrac{1}{x^3} \biggr| + C \)

    解説：

    \( \hspace{35px} \begin{eqnarray} \displaystyle \int \biggl( \dfrac{x}{4x^3 - 1} \biggr)^2\log(x^4 - x) dx & = & \displaystyle \int \dfrac{x^2}{(4x^3 - 1)^2}\log(x^4 - x) dx \\ & = & \displaystyle \int -\dfrac{1}{12}\biggl( \dfrac{1}{4x^3 - 1} \biggr)'\log(x^4 - x) dx \\ & = & -\dfrac{1}{12}\biggl( \dfrac{1}{4x^3 - 1} \biggr)\log(x^4 - x) + \dfrac{1}{12}\displaystyle \int \biggl( \dfrac{1}{4x^3 - 1} \biggr)\dfrac{4x^3 - 1}{x^4 - x} dx \\ & = & \biggl( \dfrac{1}{12 - 48x^3} \biggr)\log(x^4 - x) +\dfrac{1}{12}\displaystyle \int \dfrac{1}{x^4 - x} dx \\ & = & \biggl( \dfrac{1}{12 - 48x^3} \biggr)\log(x^4 - x) +\dfrac{1}{36}\displaystyle \int \dfrac{\dfrac{3}{x^4}}{1 - \dfrac{1}{x^3}} dx \\ & = & \biggl( \dfrac{1}{12 - 48x^3} \biggr)\log(x^4 - x) +\dfrac{1}{36}\log\biggl| 1 - \dfrac{1}{x^3} \biggr| + C \end{eqnarray} \)

  • (5)の答え：\( \dfrac{4}{3}\biggl( \dfrac{1 + \log 2}{1 - \log 2}\sqrt{\dfrac{1 + \log 2}{1 - \log 2}} - 1 \biggr) \)

    解説：

    \( t = \sqrt{\dfrac{1 + \log x}{1 - \log x}} \)とおくと、\( (1 - \log x)t^2 = (1 + \log x) \Leftrightarrow (t^2 + 1)\log x = t^2 - 1 \Leftrightarrow \log x = \dfrac{t^2 - 1}{t^2 + 1} \)
    \( 1 - \log x = \dfrac{2}{t^2 + 1} \) より \( -\dfrac{1}{x}dx = \dfrac{-4t}{(t^2 + 1)^2} dt \Leftrightarrow \dfrac{1}{x}dx = \dfrac{4t}{(t^2 + 1)^2} dt \)
    \( \therefore \dfrac{1}{1 - \log x} = t^2 + 1 \)

    \( \hspace{35px} \begin{eqnarray} \displaystyle \int_{1}^{2} \dfrac{1}{x(1 - \log x)^2}\sqrt{\dfrac{1 + \log x}{1 - \log x}} dx & = & \displaystyle \int_{1}^{\sqrt{\frac{1 + \log 2}{1 - \log 2}}} (t^2 + 1)^2t \dfrac{4t}{(t^2 + 1)^2} dt \\ & = & \displaystyle \int_{1}^{\sqrt{\frac{1 + \log 2}{1 - \log 2}}} 4t^2 dt \\ & = & \left[ \dfrac{4}{3}t^3 \right]_{1}^{\sqrt{\frac{1 + \log 2}{1 - \log 2}}} \\ & = & \dfrac{4}{3}\biggl( \dfrac{1 + \log 2}{1 - \log 2}\sqrt{\dfrac{1 + \log 2}{1 - \log 2}} - 1 \biggr) \end{eqnarray} \)
