  • (2) \( a_1 = 8,　a_{n + 1} = \dfrac{5a_n - 12}{a_n - 3} \)

  • (3) \( a_1 = \dfrac{3}{2},　a_{n + 1} = \dfrac{3}{(n + 1)(n + 2)}a_n \)

  • (4) \( a_1 = 0,　a_{n + 1} = 2a_n + n^3 \)

  • (Ex) \( a_1 = e,　a_{n + 1} = \dfrac{1}{n}\sqrt[n]{\dfrac{(2n)!}{n!}}a_n \) のとき、 \( \displaystyle \lim_{n \to \infty} a_n \) を求めよ。
