# 微分の基本 - 解答

1. $-\cos x$

2. $\dfrac{1}{x}$

3. $10x$

4. $2 \cdot 3^{2x}\log 3$

    !!! note "解説"
        $\begin{align}
        y & = 3^{2x} \\
        y' & = (2x)' \cdot 3^{2x}\log 3 \\
        & = 2 \cdot 3^{2x}\log 3
        \end{align}$

5. $x^{-\frac{3}{4}}$

    !!! note "解説"
        $\begin{align}
        y & = 4x^{0.25} \\
        y' & = 4x^{\frac{1}{4}} \\
        & = x^{-\frac{3}{4}}
        \end{align}$
